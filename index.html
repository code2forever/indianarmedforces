Our Three Forces

Our almighty power is divided into : Indian Army - Indian Navy - Indian Air Force

Indian Army

"Sewa Paramo Dharma". The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is the Supreme Commander Of the Indian Army, and its professional head is the Chief of Army Staff, who is a four-star general.

Learn More

Indian Navy

"Sham-no Varuna". The Indian Navy is the naval branch of the Indian Armed Forces. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Navy. The Chief of Naval Staff, a four-star admiral, commands the navy.

Learn More

Indian Air Force

"Nabha sparsham deeptam". The Indian Air Force is the air arm of the Indian Armed Forces. Its complement of personnel and aircraft assets ranks fourth amongst the air forces of the world. Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and to conduct aerial warfare during armed conflict.
